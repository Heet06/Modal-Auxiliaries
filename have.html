Have to/Has to/Had to

(a)

'Have to'/'Has to'/'Had to'are used as an equivalent of 'must'.'Had to'is the past tanse equivalent of 'must'.

Examples:-

1. I have to work hard to get first class this time.

2. She has to reach the station in time otherwise she will miss the bus.

3. They had to catch a bus as the taxi drivers were on a strike.
