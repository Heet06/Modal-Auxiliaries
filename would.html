Would

(a) 'Would' is used as past form of 'will'/'shall' in indirect speech.

Example:-

1. She said to me, "He'll give me some more chocolates".

2. She told me that he would give her some more chocolates.

(b)'Would' is used to make a polite requst in a question sentence.

Example:-

Would you like to have coffee?

(c) 'Would' is used to express desire and preference.

Example:-

I would prefer tea or coffee.

(d) 'Would' is used in conditional sentences.

Example:-

They would hire a cab if they did not have a car with them at that time.
