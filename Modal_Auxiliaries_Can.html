~CAN~

                                                                              

(a)  'Can' is used to express ability.It means 'am/is/are+able to'

Example:~

1. You can read German.(Meaning:I am able to read German.)
2. He can carry that heavy bag.(Meaning:He is able to carry that heavy bag.)

(b)  'Can' is sometimes used to give permission.It means one is allowed to do something.

Example:~

1. You can go now.
2. You can come any time.

(c)  'Can' is used to show the possibility of something happening.

Example:~

1. He can be at the crunch.
2. She can be in the bus.

(d)  'Can' is used to show conditions.

Example:~

1. Anyone can learn to ride a bike, if they are not afraid to try.

(e)  'Can' is used to show a polite request.

Example:~

1. can you teach me to ride a bike?

(f)  'Can' is used to show willingness.

Example:~

1. I can teach you.

(g)  'Can' is used to show probability.

Example:~
