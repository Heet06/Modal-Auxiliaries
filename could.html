~COULD~

(a)

COULD' indicates the ability to do something. it is used to express what one was able to do in the past.

Examples;-

1.amita could read french when she was in europe.

2.abhay could run five kilometers when his age was twenty.

(b)

'COULD'is also used in questions to make polite requests.

Examples;-

1.could you open that that window, please?

2.could you please hrlp me in this matter?

(c)

'COULD'is used to show possibility.

Example;-

Bill could be the best bike rider with some more practice.

(d)

'COULD'is used to make suggestion too.

Example;-

Mohan could ride his cycle to campus, instead of driving his car.
