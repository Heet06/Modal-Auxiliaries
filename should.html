Should

(a)

'Should' is used to show obligation, advice or an assumption.

Example

1. You should be more careful this time.

2. You should'nt carry pet animals in the restaurant.

3. They should be there at this time.


(b)

'Should' is used to express a sense of duty or desirability.

Example:-

1. We Should help the needy.

Peter Should pay his fees in time.

The children should obey their parents and teachers.


(c)

'Should' is used to show expectations.

Example:-

1. They shouldhave done it by that time.
