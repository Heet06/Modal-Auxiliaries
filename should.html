Should

(A) 'Shold' is used to show obligation, advice or an assumption.

Example

  • You should be more careful this time.
  • You should'nt carry pet animals in the restaurant.
  • They should be there at this time.

    • (B)'Should' is used to express a sense of duty or desirability.

    Example

  • We Should help the needy.
  • Peter Should pay his fees in time.
  • The children should obey their parents and teachers.

    • (C) 'Should' is used to show expectations.

    Example

  • They shouldhave done it by that time.
