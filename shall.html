" Shall, Will "



(a)

'Shall' and 'Will' are used for the future. The future event may be a result of our intention, wish, determination, promise or willingness.

Examples:-

1. I Will go to the zoo next sunday. (future plan)

2. You Shall do as you are told. (obligation)

3. No one shall carry any explosives in the train carriage.(prohibition)



(b)

'Will' is used to make a plite request in a question, while 'shall' is used to make suggestion.

Examples:-

1. will you clean this table, please?

2. shall i bring your book?



(c)

'Will' is used to show characteristics habits.


Example:-

1.He will talk about nothing but movies.



(d)

'Will' is used to show assumption also.

Example:-

1.He will be the clerk, I think.



(e)

'Will' is used to show promose.

Example:-

1.I will surely help you in that matter at a right time.
