~Might~

(A) 'Might' is the past tense of 'may'.

Example:
I told you that he might come with us.

(B) 'Might' is also used in present to show possibility in the future, but not certanly.

Examples:
1. He might win the race next time.
2. He might help her in that case.

(C) 'Might' is used to show suggestions.

Example:
You might want to lower the seat so that your legs reach the pedals.
