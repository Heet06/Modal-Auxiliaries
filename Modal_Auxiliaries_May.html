~MUST~

                                                                              

(a)  'Must' is used to express necessary or obligation.

Example:~

1. We must have a raincoat as it's raining.(necessary)

(b)  'Must' is also used for inference or probability.

Example:~

1. He must be at least thirty five years old.

(c)  'Must' is used to express immediate or future obligation or necessity.

Example:~

1. We must be careful while driving.

(d)  'Must' is used to express recommendation.

Example:~

1. You Must watch it. It's the best film I've ever seen.

(e)  'Must' is used to show certainly.

Example:~

1. There is no one in the hall. All must have gone now.
