~MAY~

                                                                              

(a)  'May' is used to show probability.

Example:~

1. There are black clouds. It may rain today.
2. He may come today.

(b)  'May' is used to make a polite request in a question.give permission.It means one is allowed to do something.

Example:~

1. May I come in , sir?
2. May I help you, ma'am?

(c)  'May' is used to express a wish or to give a blessing.

Example:~

1. May god bless you , my son!
2. May you live long!

(d)  'May' is used to express possibility.

Example:~

1. She may come with us for the meeting.
2. He may be able to help you for your project.

(e)  'May' is used to show predictions.

Example:~

1. They may call tomorrow.I hope so.
2. He may tell me tomorrow.
