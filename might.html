Might

(a)

'Might' is the past tense of 'may'.

Example:

1.I told you that he might come with us.

(b)

'Might' is also used in present to show possibility in the future, but not certanly.

Examples:-

1.He might win the race next time.

2.He might help her in that case.

(c)

'Might' is used to show suggestions.

Example:

1.You might want to lower the seat so that your legs reach the pedals.
